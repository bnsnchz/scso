New Celebrations: Folk Roots & Fresh Horizons

Our theme this 2025-2026 season is centered around celebrating the musical roots of our orchestra and our community, starting with a celebration of home.

Inspired by the beauty of his home in Bohemia, Smetana’s Moldau evokes the beauty of its countryside, Borodin’s bold and lyrical Symphony No. 2 draws on the richness of the composer's Russian roots, and the new work Vampire Waltz by composer Nathaniel Edison celebrates a fresh voice from our community. Bookended by works inspired by identity and home, this concert also marks our new conductor Martín Alexander Arellano’s own homecoming to California after a decade away.

Don't miss the exciting inaugural concert of our new season, celebrating home and music here in the Santa Clarita Valley.
